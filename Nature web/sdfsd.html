Nature of the Pure

We can’t live without fresh air,
clean water, sunshine and a lot of things which we take from the nature.
That's why we must take care of it. We must keep our rivers and lakes,
forests and towns clean. We must take care of each plant and each animal.
We must plant flowers — not pick them up, feed birds and animals in winter — not kill them.
Then we'll be happy to live on the most beautiful planet in the Universe.
